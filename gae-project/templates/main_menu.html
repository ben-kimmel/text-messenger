General info

This app is meant to be a fun playground for making your own super awesome text messenger tool using Twilio. The goal is to show you how to integrate text messsage communicaiton into your projects, if you have interest, how to schedule events, and how to use features like cron jobs and task queues.

Twilio is a cloud communications 'Platform as a Service' (PaaS) that helps make the jump from web apps to phone communications (text messages and phone calls) easier. It's fun to learn the basics then add your own fun features. In order to use this app you need to make an account on Twilio. You'll need to invest $5 into your Twilio account.

** Update ** Twilio now has a Free Trial program, so you can test without putting in any funds!

Use the link below to create an account with Twillio, then come back to this page for the next steps (listed below).

Create an account on Twilio

Getting your Twilio account setup

** Update ** Twilio now has a Free Trial program, so you can test without putting in any funds! You will still need to do step #3 below, but you can just follow the Twilio Get Started links to click "Get your first Twilio phone number". The Free Trial will give you a phone number fo free now!

1
Adding $5 to your Twilio balance

You should've made your account with Twilio from the step above, now you need to visit your Twilio console https://www.twilio.com/console and add $5 to your account. Use the Billing Tab (upper left), then select Add Funds. The min you can add is $5 which should be enough for this demo. I recommend you use Paypal (set up a Paypal account if needed, you'll be glad you did). If you continue to use the app you may want to add more than $5, but $5 will get you about 4 months worth of playing with Twilio.

2
Buy a Twilio number

On the Twilio console click on the Dashboard (upper left) and find where it says "Buy a Number". Click it and select your very own phone number. It costs $1 per month to own this number, which is why $5 will last you about 4 months. You will need that number in the next step.

3
Now register your Twilio account with this app
Click the link below to visit your Settings page. On the settings page you will enter your Twilio account info. You need three pieces of information ready, which can be found on your Twilio console Dashboard:
  • ACCOUNT SID - Available as the top item on your Twilio account summary
  • AUTH TOKEN - Available as the second item on your Twilio account summary (need to click the lock icon to see your Auth Token)
  • Phone number - The number you just purchased, in E.164 number format, which looks like this +18122016488 not like this (812) 201-6488.
    • More information about the E.164 number format can be found here.
    • Additionally Twilio has a slick tool for figuring out the E.164 number format for any number in the world here.
Visit your Settings page

Twilio Automatic responders

Additionally on your settings page you may have noticed the fields for automatic responses to phone calls and SMS messages. Adding text to those fields will only do something if you setup the Webhook within your Twilio console. Within the Twilio console for your phone number, set your auto voice and auto SMS responders as follows:
  • Voice Webhook ==> http://yourusername-textmessenger.appspot.com/voice-auto-reply
  • Messaging Webhook ==> http://yourusername-textmessenger.appspot.com/sms-auto-reply
Use the link below to visit your phone number(s) console. Then click on the phone number and add the webhooks shown above (replacing yourusername as appropriate).
Visit your Twilio console for phone numbers

Contacts

You are done with the hard part (Twilio account setup). Now you need enter a bunch of Contacts that might receive text messages. Use the link below to visit the Contacts page (or the link at the top of any page). When you add contact make sure to use the E.164 number format for all of their phone numbers, which looks like this +18122016488 not like this (812) 201-6488. See more info about about the E.164 number format.

Text Messages Events

Finally you need to be able to schedule text message events. An event is simply a time when the message will send, to whom, and text for the message. Text Message Events can send right now or be scheduled for a later date / time.
